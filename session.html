5 Minute Focus Boost 🌿

Meditation: Nadi Shodhana

Benefits

Reduces anxiety, improves focus, balances the nervous system, and prepares the mind for studying.

Contraindications

Avoid forceful breathing. Do not practice if severely congested. Consult a doctor if you have respiratory issues.

Posture & Hand Position

Sit comfortably with spine straight.

Right hand: Fold index and middle fingers inward. Use thumb to close right nostril. Use ring finger to close left nostril.
